Product Details

The Vorrin pants are a striking avant-garde statement piece, crafted from what appears to be burlap, jute, or linen fabric. These oversized, wide-leg pants feature dramatic ruched and ruffled edges, creating a sculptural silhouette that exudes artistic and high-fashion appeal. With a drawstring or tie-up waist, they maintain a raw, organic aesthetic, making them perfect for those who appreciate unique, unconventional fashion. Ideal for styling with a minimal crop top or a structured jacket, the Vorrin pants offer a bold and sophisticated look.
